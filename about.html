About Dark Thunder Games

Dark Thunder Games is a creative game development team founded in April 2022. Our mission is to craft fun and enjoyable games for players of all kinds.

Our History

Originally known as Goofy Games Studios, the team spent its early years developing games using Julians Editor and Scratch. During this time, we focused mainly on simple clicker games and short, humorous projects.

In late 2023, we decided to take a new direction to improve the quality and creativity of our games. This led to a rebranding, and on June 17th, 2024, Goofy Games Studios officially became Dark Thunder Games.
